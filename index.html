Frequently Asked Questions

Here you will find answers to the most common inquiries about our orthopedic braces.

What types of orthopedic braces do you offer?

We offer a wide range of braces, including knee braces, back supports, wrist braces, ankle braces, and custom-made braces to suit individual needs.

How do I know which brace is right for me?

Our specialists will assess your condition and recommend the most suitable brace for your needs, whether it’s for injury recovery, mobility support, or pain relief.

Do you offer custom braces?

Yes, we provide custom braces designed specifically to fit your body and address your unique requirements.

How can I schedule a fitting or consultation?

You can book a fitting or consultation appointment through our website or by calling our office directly. Early booking is recommended.

Are your braces covered by insurance?

Many insurance plans cover our orthopedic braces. We recommend contacting your insurance provider to confirm coverage details.

What if I need adjustments or repairs for my brace?

If your brace requires adjustments or repairs, our team is available to help. Contact us to schedule a service appointment.
